About Me

My name is Anniina Salow and I am studying Information Processing Science at the University of Oulu. I am fascinated by everything you can do with a computer. When comes to my studies, my interests are mostly in software testing, HCI, UX/UI, digitalisation, and big data. I am looking forward to working with these subjects in future. My interest in HCI sparked me to support my knowledge by studying psychology. My programming knowledge is mostly with Python, HTML and CSS but I also know the basics with C and Java. I have created two websites with HTML, CSS and JavaScript and used Figma as a prototyping tool. For my master's thesis, I created a data retrieval program with Python to retrieve specific data from big data and then visualized the data with Power BI.


As an employee, I am hardworking and I highly appreciate both working in a team and a good working environment. I am at my best when I work under pressure hence I seek challenges in my career. Being a problem solver comes naturally to me and I want to always do my job well. These features show in my work and in the feedback I have gotten. I have always known that my future is with ICT and that dream still stands.
