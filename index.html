About Me

My name is Anniina Salow and I graduated from University of Oulu on May, 2024, with a master’s degree in Information Processing Science. I am a problem solver to my nature and interested in the continuously developing world. My interests in the field are HCI, software testing, mobile networks, front-end development and big data, and I am looking forward to working with these subjects in future. My programming knowledge is mostly with Python, HTML and CSS but I also know the basics with C and Java.


With nearly three years of experience in manual and automated software testing at Nokia, I have developed a critical mind and learned how to test software performance at its limits. Despite my previous trainee title, I have taken on the responsibilities of a full-time test engineer and had been involved with extremely high-priority cases. I have learned to ensure software quality and to notice the smallest issues in software performance. I have been awarded several times for my contributions and successes in my testing tasks and I am humbled by the positive feedback I have received from my colleagues. I have always said yes to new, more challenging opportunities and delivered high-quality results. Even though I had the opportunity to work as a software tester, I also had the chance to get to explore data mining and artificial intelligence through my thesis, supervised by a leading expert in the field. I created a sophisticated program which goes through a massive database, restructuring data into a format compatible with a data visualization tool. The program is part of an automation and AI system and runs daily to provide up-to-date data for management. The program isn’t just reading from a file; it mines the database and creates a whole new index to be able to use for specific management reports now and in the future.


As an employee, I am hardworking and I highly appreciate both working in a team and a good working environment. I am at my best when I work under pressure hence I seek challenges in my career. I want to always do my job well and developing my skills in this industry is something I am eager to continue. These features show in my work and in the feedback I have gotten. I have always known that my future is with ICT and that dream still stands.
